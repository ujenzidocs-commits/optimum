                    YOUTUBE VIDEO URL FORMATS
================================================================================

SUPPORTED FORMATS:
  ✅ https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VIDEO_ID
  ✅ https://youtu.be/VIDEO_ID
  ✅ https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VIDEO_ID&t=10s (with timestamps)
  ✅ VIDEO_ID (direct 11-character ID)

EXAMPLE:
  Full:     https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ
  Short:    https://youtu.be/dQw4w9WgXcQ
  ID:       dQw4w9WgXcQ

VIDEO PLAYER CONTROLS:
  • Play/Pause: Click or Spacebar
  • Volume: Mouse wheel or slider
  • Fullscreen: Double-click or button
  • Seek: Timeline or arrow keys
  • Mute: 'M' key
  • Captions: 'C' key

     - Full blog title

     - Author information
